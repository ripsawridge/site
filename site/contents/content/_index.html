I'm hiking the Continental Divide Trail, starting April 21st!

I'm already blogging about it at Getting-Lost-On-The-CDT.blogspot.com. I'm traveling light, and traveling alone. My thought is, if I deepen the wilderness outside of me, then I may enter a vaster space inside. As a kid, the deserts of New Mexico were a magical place my mom and I drove through, stopping at ghost towns. The main feeling was "wonderment." Wondering at the lives lived out here...the arc of them, from bright promise to faded glory.

Perhaps we spring up in a desert as well. Maybe we already see that our reach exceeded our grasp. But there is beauty in acceptance. Look: the desert flower perishes. It is amazing, that it began at all. In the heart of this mystery, I will walk.
