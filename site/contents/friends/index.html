My friends

I've tagged my reports with friends and family that come along. With this feature, I've been able to create a custom URL for each person I climbed with, this way they can see the reports that contain them. I hope to enhance that with statistics, though, I guess, this is not that useful if you do things with people other than myself! But I'm thinking here of my girlfriend, for whom it will be a pretty complete record of her work in the hills. And that sounds fun to me.
