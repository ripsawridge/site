Welcome to my site

If we find ourselves with a desire that nothing in this world can satisfy,
the most probable explanation is that we were made for another world.
--C. S. Lewis

First the traditional stuff...the way I staked out my identity for so long I risk freezing into it...trip reports and statistics about climbing. Beyond that I have many interests.

I quit using social media platforms, not liking the effect they had on my thought process (Twitter remains for my V8 work). But I still like to "shout out" sometimes about things I'm doing. The funny thing is, it's actually better for me if no one hears me. So, I keep a humble log of books I read or music I wrote and such things here. If it's a longer thought I'll write up a blog post about it, and if it's a mountain climb why, by God it'll appear on the mountain page!
